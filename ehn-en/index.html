FIELD NOTES . THE PROBLEM IN FOUR PANELS
Faced with a quote, everyone makes the same face.
This is not just your problem.
1 . IT ARRIVES
A quote arrives. A column of numbers. No way to tell if it is high or fair.
2 . YOU COMPARE
You gather three quotes. All different. You compared, and understand less.
3 . YOU ASK
You ask online. Sympathy gathers. But the answer never comes.
4 . SO YOU DECIDE
You decide on a hunch.
You have no ruler.
So I built a newspaper.
