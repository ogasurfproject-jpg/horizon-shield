Why us

The system won't

I left school as a teenager and became a carpenter. Thirty years on site. A year ago I taught myself to code, with AI as my teacher. I'm not a politician. I'm not a lawyer. I'm one person who got tired of watching people get overcharged — and built the tool to stop it. If one ex-carpenter can do this, ordinary people can change the rest.
